Spaceship Captain

Planet Labs is looking for a talented, well-rounded scientist-engineer to pilot its growing fleet of satellites.

This is a opportunity to take responsibility for dozens of satellites in orbit, to build a system that scales to hundreds of satellites, and to work in an exciting growing company at the forefront of aerospace technology development.

Desirable experiences:
  • Past experience designing and/or operating remote-controlled or robotic systems
  • Basic knowledge of electronics
  • Basic understanding of aerospace concepts such as orbital dynamics, attitude determination and control, power subsystems or willingness/ability to quickly learn this.
  • Mathematical background, particularly with discrete math and combinatorial optimization problems such as scheduling and deconfliction

Robot Counsellor

As robots start to play even larger roles in our lives, wealthy people will own robots who act as servants or caregivers. People looking to purchase robots for their homes will work with robot counselors to determine which model is best suited to a family's particular needs. If a robot does not fit in with the family, the counsellor will be on hand to determine better options and to provide care and customer service.
